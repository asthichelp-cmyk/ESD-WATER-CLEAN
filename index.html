💡 Solutions & Best Practices

🌧️

Rainwater Harvesting

Collecting and storing rainwater for domestic use reduces dependency on groundwater and helps in drought-prone regions.

☀️

Solar Disinfection (SODIS)

Exposing clear PET bottles to direct sunlight for 6+ hours kills pathogens — a low-cost method for rural communities.

🪴

Constructed Wetlands

Natural wetland plants filter and treat wastewater biologically — a sustainable alternative to chemical treatment plants.

🤝

Community-Led Sanitation (CLTS)

Empowering communities to analyse their own sanitation situation and take collective action to end open defecation.

📱

Smart Water Monitoring

IoT sensors and mobile apps can detect contamination in real-time, allowing faster response to water quality issues.
